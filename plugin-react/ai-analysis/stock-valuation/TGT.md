**DCF 計算步驟:**

1.  **預測營收 (Revenue)**
2.  **預測 EBIT (Earnings Before Interest and Taxes) = Revenue * Operating Margin**
3.  **預測稅後 EBIT (NOPAT) = EBIT * (1 - Tax Rate)**
4.  **預測資本支出 (Capital Expenditure)**
5.  **預測折舊與攤銷 (Depreciation & Amortization)**
6.  **預測營運資本變動 (Change in Net Working Capital)**
7.  **計算自由現金流 (FCF) = NOPAT + Depreciation & Amortization - Capital Expenditure - Change in Net Working Capital**
8.  **計算終值 (Terminal Value, TV)**
    *   TV = [FCF (最後一年) * (1 + g)] / (WACC - g)
9.  **將 FCF 和 TV 折現至現值 (Present Value)**
10. **計算企業價值 (Enterprise Value, EV) = 所有 FCF 現值之和 + TV 現值**
11. **計算股權價值 (Equity Value) = EV - 淨債務 (Net Debt)**
12. **計算每股價值 (Intrinsic Value per Share) = 股權價值 / 流通股數**
